Chic and Sustainable.

Our goal is to create home and lifestyle goods with a signature aesthetic that is casual and refined, and stems from the idea that what’s
beautiful and chic is always accessible. Emphasis is placed on developing products that reflect a love of quality and craftsmanship and
are either produced locally or sourced from sustainable materials.

ABOUT US

We are a Toronto-based home accessories brand celebrated for our vibrant, colorful decorative patterns that bring a unique and lively charm to any space. Established in 1996, our journey began with a focus on crafting exquisite handmade ceramic tiles. These tiles were not only functional but also showcased the intricate artistry and craftsmanship that defined our brand’s early years. As we evolved, we expanded our vision beyond ceramics, shifting our focus to creating stylish tableware that seamlessly blends functionality with aesthetic appeal. Each piece in our collection is thoughtfully designed to elevate everyday dining experiences, making them as visually delightful as they are practical.

Our commitment to quality craftsmanship remains at the heart of everything we do. We take pride in our dedication to sustainability, sourcing eco-friendly materials and adopting practices that align with our values. This approach continues to resonate with consumers who share our passion for beautiful, responsibly-made home accessories. Whether it's a bold pattern or a delicate design, every piece we create carries the essence of our brand's vibrant spirit and unwavering integrity.

OUR STYLE

Our collection is thoughtfully crafted, blending simple geometric shapes with motifs inspired by various decorative movements to achieve a timeless yet versatile aesthetic. Patterns like stripes, checks, abstract dashes, and occasional floral blossoms are meticulously scaled to harmonize with one another. This cohesive design ensures that every piece complements the next, allowing customers to mix and match effortlessly to create countless combinations.

Our collection invites homeowners to embrace experimentation, empowering them to transform everyday living spaces into something truly unique. Whether used to elevate a dining table, enhance a cozy living room, or add a touch of sophistication to a bedroom, our pieces bring a sense of artistry, charm, and distinctiveness to any setting. With every carefully crafted pattern and motif, we strive to offer more than just home accessories—we provide the tools to create a space that tells a story, reflects a lifestyle, and radiates personal flair.
